Thinking Outside the Box About Turtles & Bogs

Since much of the remaining habitat for NJ's rarest turtle species occurs on private property, landowner collaboration is essential. KGA brought in Conserve Wildlife Foundation biologist Christine Healy to show what we can all do.

Box turtles — found in wooded uplands throughout NJ
Wood turtles — a species of special concern
Bog turtles — one of North America's most endangered
Financial & technical support available to landowners
